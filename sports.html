SPORTS IN DSPS, PANIPAT

Sports and games in school go beyond physical activity—they play a crucial role in boosting self-esteem,
mental alertness, and overall well-being. A balanced student life includes both academic excellence and
active participation in sports for a healthy and energetic lifestyle. Our school provides extensive playgrounds
and open spaces where students engage in a variety of sports, including volleyball, racing, kho-kho, kabaddi,
badminton, football, basketball, swimming, skating, high jump, martial arts, relay races, table tennis, shooting,
and cricket. Every student is encouraged to participate in games and competitions at house and class levels.
